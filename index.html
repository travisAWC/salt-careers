The S.A.L.T. Standard

Four non-negotiables that define who we are.

Not rules. Not suggestions. Identity. These are practiced on every site, every day. If they resonate with you, you'll feel at home here.

S

Standard of Excellence

Leave No Trace. Sharpen Your Blade.

We don't care for industry standards. We set the standard, and our standard is excellence. We leave every site cleaner than we found it. No tire tracks on the lawn, no cigarette butts in the mulch. Tight joints, crisp edges, smooth finishes. "Good enough" is not in our vocabulary — we do it right or not at all.

A

Always Take Ownership

Plumb Is Plumb. No Excuses.

A post is either vertical or it isn't. Weather, traffic, or a bad night doesn't change the standard. We say "I screwed up" first. We fix problems in 30 minutes, not 30 excuses. If you see a problem, you own it — even if you didn't cause it.

L

Lift Each Other

Words Build or Destroy. Choose Courage.

We draw out the potential in everyone, even when it's uncomfortable. Say it to their face, not behind their back. Praise publicly, correct privately. We see potential in people bigger than they see in themselves — and we tell them.

T

Trust The Process

Measure Twice, Cut Once.

Lumber and stone are expensive; questions are free. We don't guess. If you aren't 100% sure, stop and ask. Follow the SOP — every single time. Want to improve it? Great. After you complete it. No one is above the process.
